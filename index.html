Info

I'm an artist and materials specialist with prior experience exhibiting professionally and working in higher education. For the last five years I have dedicated myself to fine-art materials research and testing while maintaining a close connection to education. Being a curious person at heart, I am a problem solver who likes to understand how all manner of things work. This has led to many rabbit holes with recent interests in project management, programming, web development, and emerging technologies.

Below is a catalogue of experiences, both professional and personal that are, perhaps, more quantifiable measures of my activities and interests. For a one-pager, here is a link to my Resume.
